Solar Installation with Civil Foundation Handled In-House.

33 years of engineer-led execution in Surat. Call us directly to discuss your project, or read on for full details.

Overview

Most solar installers handle only the panels and electrical connection, leaving the civil foundation and structural mounting work to a separate contractor. We do both as one accountable scope, which removes the coordination risk between two different teams.

We install ground-mounted solar systems for larger sites and rooftop systems for residential and commercial buildings. Our civil team builds the foundation and structural mounting to handle wind load and roof weight distribution correctly, before the panels and electrical systems go in.

Whether you are a housing society evaluating common-area solar, or a commercial or industrial client looking to offset operational power costs, we manage the project from civil foundation through to final commissioning.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle the civil foundation work yourself?

Yes. Unlike installers who only handle panels, our civil team builds the structural foundation and mounting in-house, so there is one accountable team for the entire installation.

Is my rooftop suitable for solar without structural reinforcement?

It depends on your existing roof's load capacity. Our engineer assesses this during the site visit and will recommend reinforcement only if genuinely needed.
