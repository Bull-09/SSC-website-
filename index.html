Who We Are

A Name Built Over Three Decades of Delivering What Others Only Promise.

Siddh Sai Corporation was founded in 1993 by Mr. Sanjay Shinkar with one principle: every structure we touch must be stronger, safer, and more durable than the day we found it.

Over 33 years, we have laid Surat's bitumen and RCC roads, installed thousands of square metres of paver blocks, waterproofed hundreds of residential terraces and commercial basements, and rebuilt homes from their very foundations.
