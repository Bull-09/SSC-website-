Overview

A roof in Surat has to do more work than in most cities: it needs to handle intense monsoon rainfall, summer heat, and years of UV exposure without failing. We install, repair, and fully replace residential and commercial roofing systems with this specific climate in mind.

We work across RCC slab roofing, metal sheet roofing for sheds and industrial spaces, and tiled roofing for residential properties. Every roofing job starts with an assessment of the existing structure, drainage slope, and any underlying moisture damage before we proceed.

Where a roof has existing leaks, we do not simply install new material over the problem. We identify and resolve the root cause, often in coordination with our waterproofing team, before any new roofing material goes down.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need a repair or a full replacement?

Our engineer inspects the extent of damage, the age of the existing roof, and the underlying structure. If damage is localized, repair is usually sufficient. Widespread deterioration typically calls for replacement.

Do you also handle waterproofing for roofs?

Yes. Roofing and waterproofing are closely connected, and where leaks are involved, we coordinate both services as one project so the root cause is fully addressed.
