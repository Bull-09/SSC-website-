Overview

Paint quality is determined far more by surface preparation than by the paint itself. We treat crack filling, sanding, priming, and putty work as the most important steps in any painting project, because skipping them is the single biggest reason paint jobs fail early.

We execute interior and exterior painting across standard emulsion, premium texture finishes, and enamel work for doors, grills, and metal surfaces. For exterior work in particular, we account for Surat's monsoon exposure and select weatherproof exterior emulsions rated for the climate.

Every painting project includes proper masking and protection of flooring, fixtures, and furniture, and a final walkthrough with you before we consider the job complete.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you handle painting around existing furniture?

We mask and cover all furniture and fixtures before work begins. For larger rooms, we can also work in sections so part of the space remains usable.

What is the difference between exterior and interior paint?

Exterior paints are formulated to resist UV exposure, rain, and temperature swings. Using interior paint outdoors leads to early peeling and fading, especially in Surat's monsoon conditions.
