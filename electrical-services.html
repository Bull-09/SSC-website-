Overview

Electrical work is not an area where shortcuts are acceptable, the cost of a mistake is too high. Every electrical job we execute, from full home wiring to a single panel upgrade, follows IS standard specifications and is tested before we consider it complete.

We handle full residential and commercial wiring for new construction and renovation, panel and MCB upgrades for older properties, switchboard and socket installation, and comprehensive electrical safety audits for properties with ageing wiring.

For renovation clients in particular, we often find that old wiring is undersized for modern appliance loads. We flag this during our assessment so you can make an informed decision before finishing walls and ceilings over it.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my home's wiring needs an upgrade?

Frequent tripping, warm switchboards, flickering lights, or wiring older than 15-20 years are all signs worth getting assessed. We offer a safety audit to give you a clear answer.

Can electrical work be done after flooring and painting are finished?

It is always better to plan electrical work before finishing work, since wiring often needs to run through walls and ceilings. We recommend involving our electrical team early in your renovation planning.
