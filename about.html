Where We Started

Where We Started. Who We Are Now.

Siddh Sai Corporation was established in 1993 by Mr. Sanjay Shinkar, a civil engineer with an unwavering belief that the construction industry in Surat deserved better: better materials, better methods, and most importantly, better accountability.

In the early years, the company focused primarily on road infrastructure, laying bitumen and RCC roads for housing societies and private clients across Surat. The work was physically demanding, technically precise, and unforgiving of error. It built the discipline that still defines every project we take on today.

As Surat grew into one of India's fastest-developing cities, so did the complexity of what our clients needed. The 2000s brought a surge in residential construction. The 2010s brought demand for premium renovation, waterproofing, and turnkey technical services. Through each decade, Siddh Sai Corporation evolved its equipment, its team, and its capabilities, while never changing the foundational principle that a project is only complete when the client is genuinely satisfied.

Today, under the continued leadership of Mr. Sanjay Shinkar and Mr. Lokesh Shinkar, we serve both Surat's infrastructure needs and the renovation ambitions of its most discerning homeowners, with the same team-led, engineer-supervised approach that built our reputation across 33 years.

Our Principles

The Standards That Have Protected Our Reputation for 33 Years.

Engineering Discipline

Every project we accept is first assessed by an engineer. Every material choice is justified by structural logic, not margin optimization. We do not guess. We calculate.

Accountability Without Excuses

If a project encounters a problem under our watch, our team resolves it without billing debates or contractor deflection. Our name is attached to every structure we touch.

No Compromises on Material Quality

We source ISI-certified materials from established manufacturers. We do not substitute without client knowledge and consent. The materials we specify are the materials we use.

Transparent Pricing. Always.

Our estimates are line-item, not lump-sum. You know what you are paying for before we begin. Change orders are documented and approved. You will never receive a surprise invoice.

Modern Equipment, Proven Methods

We own our heavy machinery and keep it maintained. This means we control project timelines, quality, and cost rather than being dependent on sub-contractors with misaligned incentives.

Surat Is Our Home

Every project we complete exists in the city we live and operate in. Our reputation is built road by road, home by home, across a community where word of mouth still matters most.
